Ecuador

Ecuador is a country defined by its striking geographical diversity, stretching from the sunny Pacific coast and the lush, biodiverse Amazon rainforest to the towering, snow-capped peaks of the Andes Mountains. Sitting right on the equator, it enjoys a rich variety of microclimates and is home to incredibly unique ecosystems, including the famous volcanic Galápagos Islands. This natural wealth is beautifully matched by vibrant cultural heritage, blending indigenous traditions with colonial history in bustling cities and peaceful mountain towns alike.
