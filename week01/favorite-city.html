My Favorite City

Manta is a truly remarkable coastal city, and there are two aspects that genuinely stand out:

First, its seamless integration of urban life and beautiful coastline is exceptional. Unlike many destinations where you have to travel far to escape the city, Manta allows you to transition from a bustling commercial center or a fine dinner straight onto the sands of Playa El Murciélago in just a matter of minutes. It gives the city a distinct, refreshing energy where business and coastal relaxation coexist perfectly.

Second, the culinary scene, particularly regarding its seafood, is world-class. Because Manta is a major Pacific port, the seafood is unparalleled in its freshness. Sampling the local encebollado or a traditional Manabí-style ceviche is practically a requirement; the local chefs have an incredible way of honoring traditional flavors while showcasing the daily catch. It is a true highlight of any visit.
